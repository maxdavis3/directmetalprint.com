CAPABILITIES

Direct Metal Printing Technology

01

Laser Metal Fusion

500W+ fiber laser fully melts metal powder at each voxel. Complete melt pool ensures 99.5% density without binders — direct path from powder to structural metal part.

02

Inert Atmosphere Control

Argon atmosphere maintained at <10 ppm oxygen eliminates oxidation during printing — critical for titanium, Inconel, and other reactive metal alloy systems.

03

Powder Recovery

Unfused powder is sieved and reused, reducing material waste and per-part cost. Full powder traceability maintained for aerospace and medical batch records.

CAPABILITIES

Direct Melting Advantages

Direct melting — not sintering — delivers forged-equivalent microstructure and mechanical properties in any complex geometry.

  • ● Full melt pool — no green strength phase needed
  • ● No post-print sintering furnace step required
  • ● Grain structure comparable to forged material
  • ● HIP optional: final porosity elimination to 100%
  • ● All six alloys: full-density capability proven
  • ● Rapid parameter qualification for new materials
